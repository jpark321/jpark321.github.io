Hello World! I am MSc student in the Computer Science department at University of Illinois Springfield. Prior to joining UIS, I earned my BSc in Mathematics from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a minor in CS.

I was fortunate enough to have multiple internship opportunities at various companies. In Summer 2022, I interned at Encyclopædia Britannica to implement better search strategy on articles using Sphinx search engine. Later that Fall, I interned at AWS Lambda's Capacity Orchestration team to make improvements on its concurrency limit management system to factor in customers' Graviton processor concurrency usage.

My research interests mostly revolve around (but certainly are not limited to) machine learning on texts: Natural Language Processing/Understanding. I also am keen to delve into graph neural networks and temporal neural networks, searching for ways to incorporate these concepts to solving NLP tasks.

I hope to log steps of my journey here and showcase my knowledge/experiments.

Thank you for spending the time to stop by.

Best regards,
